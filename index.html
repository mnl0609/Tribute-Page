Here's a time line of President Rodrigo Duterte's current life:

  • 1945 - Born in Maasin, Philippines
  • 1968 - Duterte earned a political science degree from Lyceum of the Philippines University in Manila specifically in San Beda College.
  • 1972 - Earned a law degree from San Beda College as well.
  • 1977 - Duterte joined the Davao City prosecutor’s office.
  • 1986 - Duterte was appointed to be the Vice Mayor of the City of Davao.
  • 1988 - Rodrigo Duterte was finally elected to be a Mayor of the City. The first ever election he was appointed but unsuccessfully won the election. However, there was a re-election made (twice to be precise).
  • 1998-2001 - The re-election happened within this timeframe and was successfully became the Mayor of Davao City.
  • 2010 - Because of the term-limit, Rodrigo Duterte then became the Vice Mayor of Davao City and his daughter Sara Duterte, was elected to be the Mayor of the City.
  • 2013 - Duterte returned to the mayor’s office, this time with his son Paolo (“Pulong”) serving as vice mayor.
  • 2016 - cDuterte was inaugurated as president of the Philippines. In his first six months in office, more than 6,000 people were killed in Duterte’s “war on drugs.”
  • 2017 - Duterte met with Russian Pres. Vladimir Putin to discuss the prospect of an arms deal. While Duterte was in Moscow, a series of deadly clashes erupted in Marawi between Filipino troops and Islamist fighters linked to the Islamic State in Iraq and the Levant (ISIL; also called ISIS).
  • 2018 - he International Criminal Court (ICC) opened a preliminary investigation into the more than 12,000 deaths that had occurred during Duterte’s “war on drugs.”
  • 2019 - Although government forces retook Marawi and quashed the rebellion, the declaration was renewed through the end of 2019, making it the longest period of martial law in the Philippines since the Marcos era.
